About System

An online voting system is a digital platform that allows individuals to cast their votes electronically, providing convenience and accessibility.

System's Services

Online voting systems provide several services to facilitate the voting process. Here are some common services offered by online voting systems:

  • Ballot creation and management: Online voting systems allow organizations to create and manage ballots easily. This includes the ability to add, remove, and edit ballot items, as well as to set deadlines and permissions.
  • Voter registration and authentication: Online voting systems can help organizations to register voters and authenticate their identities. This can be done using a variety of methods, such as email verification, SMS verification, or two-factor authentication.
  • Voting: Online voting systems allow voters to cast their votes securely and conveniently. This can be done from any device with an internet connection.
  • Vote counting and reporting: Online voting systems can automatically count votes and generate reports on the results. This can help to ensure that elections are conducted fairly and transparently.
  • Auditability: Online voting systems should be designed to be auditable. This means that the results of an election can be verified by an independent party.
